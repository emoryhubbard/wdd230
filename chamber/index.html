News Release

The White Oak shopping center will soon have a Walmart Supercenter, which will occupy the same area as the current location previously occupied by Sears. The Sears, apart from its auto-care center, had closed its doors in 2018, but now new construction will begin on its former building in order to support the infrastructure for the new supercenter. The rest of the White Oak shopping center will remain accessible, as the Sears site was positioned on the far end of the shopping center adjacent to New Hampshire Ave.
