Color Scheme

The White Oak Chamber Site includes four options for colors. These consist of primary, secondary, first accent, and second accent colors. The primary and secondary colors are based on the logo. The accent colors were chosen to be in harmony with them as well.

Primary Color

Taken from the logo color, the primary color will be #213458. It will be used for backgrounds on various elements such as the header and footer.
