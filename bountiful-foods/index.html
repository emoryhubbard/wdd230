All About San Diego

San Diego County is well known for its large population, ranking 5th in the entire nation. It is also the furthest south and west out of any other location in the United States. In addition, an exceptionally large amount of Native American reservations can be found there. There are also 16 full military bases belonging to various branches in the area. It shares a border with industrial centers in Mexico, with which it has strong economic ties. The county also has a widely varied climate, which is classified as Mediteranean in some areas and semi-arid in others.
