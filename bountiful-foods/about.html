Who We Are

At Bountiful Foods, we are constantly focusing on locally-grown and organic foods in the greater San Diego coastal area. Our headquarters are located in Carlsbad, California. To deliver our customers this food, however, requires us to cover great distances. Most of us are on the road, headed your way with excellent-quality produce. Our mission is to provide people with a high-quality, healthy, and fresh selection of produce.

Fresh, Healthy Difference

What sets us apart from the rest of the organic sellers is our custom-order specialty fruit drink and fruit bag delivery service, serving local people as well as tourists. There aren't any other companies that come close to the quality we provide with organic, locally-grown, and fresh-squeezed fruits.
